Snake Game

Rules Of The Game :

  • In mobile phones you can't play this game.
  • If you hit the walls you will lose the game.
  • If you press left arrow in your pc(or laptop) snake will turn left and if you want to move right press right arrow and your head will become tail and tail will become head .And it is similar for top and bottom also.
  • You can pass the game by pressing pass button and you can view you score on top of the board.
  • You should press start game to start your game.
